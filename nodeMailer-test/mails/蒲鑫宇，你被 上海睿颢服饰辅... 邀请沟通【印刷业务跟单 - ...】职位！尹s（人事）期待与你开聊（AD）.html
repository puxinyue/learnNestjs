职位描述
 
岗位职责：

Contact and manage client, principally handling the current vendors as well as new accounts, follow up and guide for the whole process from inquiry to shipment；联络并管理客户，主要包括现有 Vendor 以及新客户，跟进并处理客户从询价到发货的全程订单服务；

Communicate effectively with client identifying needs & opportunities. Independently handling with client from inquiry and provide professional customer service experience. 与客户保持有效的沟通，明确客户需求。独立处理客户咨询， 提供优质的客户服务；

Work closely with supporting team in order to deliver complete solution and service to customers; 与公司内部支持团队紧密合作，为客户提供完整的解决方案和服务;

Negotiate pricing and contractual agreement with supplier and customer to achieve business objectives; 与供应商和客户议价并达成合同约定，以实现业务目标；

Involved in product development & visit customer if necessary; 参与产品开发及有需要时拜访客户；

Issue sales report if needed; 有需要时协助整理销售报告；

完成领导安排的其他工作。



任职需求：

College degree or above in International Trading or other relevant disciplines; 大专 及以上学历, 国际贸易或其他相关专业；

Minimum 3 years experiences as Merchandiser in handling packaging, or garment trims & accessories products; (Less experience will be considered to Junior AE); 3 年 以上跟单的工作经验，有包装印刷或服装辅料行业相关工作经验（经验少于 3 年可应聘 Junior AE 岗位）；

Good communication skills, independent, self-motivated and willing to work under pressure; 良好的沟通表达能力，能独立完成工作，有上进心、责任心，有一定 的抗压能力;

Proficient in MS Office applications 熟练操作 office 办公软件;

Good command of English in reading and writing; 英文读写流利;

ERP knowledge is an advantage; 有 ERP 操作经验者优先;

Immediately available will be preferred; 能尽快到岗的优先。
